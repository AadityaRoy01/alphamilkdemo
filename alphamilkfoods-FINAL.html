Our Story

An Integrated Dairy Built on Trust

Since 2014, Alpha Milkfoods has built a vertically integrated dairy business that connects every link in the value chain — from village-level milk collection to global distribution. What began as a commitment to Indian farmers has grown into one of the most trusted names in the industry.

🌾
Direct Farmer Procurement

We collect raw milk directly from 15,000+ farmers across 500+ villages using state-of-the-art Milk Analyzers — ensuring freshness, fair pricing, and zero adulteration.

🏭
World-Class Processing Plant

Our Hathras facility runs fully independent Cow and Buffalo milk lines with complete end-to-end traceability, ultra-modern processing, and rigorous in-house laboratory testing.

📋
Flawless Quality Management

Every batch undergoes multi-stage testing across raw material intake, manufacturing, and finished goods — backed by FSSAI, ISO, HACCP, Halal, and BIS certifications.
